The goal of this site

The site covers the 2024 local and EP elections in Hungary. It will contain regularly updated predictions on the expected number of seats of each party or group of parties in the EP election, and it will also cover the main contests in the local election.


The Prediction methodology tab provides further information on how the predictions are made.
