Purpose of this site

This page gives voters guidance on who will be challenging Fidesz in each constituency in the 2022 general election.


The site also gives a forecast of how many seats each side is expected to win in the election, based on current polling data, and in which constituencies Fidesz or united opposition candidate is leading. For more information on the functioning of the forecast, see the Forecasting methodology tab.


Summary data for the last two parliamentary elections are available under the 2014-2018 elections tab.
